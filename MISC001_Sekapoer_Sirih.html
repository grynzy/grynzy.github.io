Sekapoer Sirih:
Motivation behind this website

To begin is the most difficult part of a learning process. To keep the consistency is the more difficult one.

My very first post here will be the introduction of what I am thinking right now regarding the notion of this website.

Hello surfers, my name is Peter. At the time of this writing, I am an Indonesian who is currently studying in Taiwan, taking Biomedical Engineering as my major. In depth, my research focuses on Orthopedic Biomechanics, where I am studying finite element analysis to enhance the stability on the knee joint in response to several disease such as ligament reconstruction and bone fracture.

Honestly, I do not have any luxurious reason about using "Grynzy" as my alias in the world of internet. It's just because I like green very much, thus I try to find a good variant name of my favorite color. Having only consonant letters seems pretty eye catching and brings game-based vibe, because I also like playing games (PES, PUBG and MotoGP are my favorites).

I am glad but quite nervous in starting this website, having no experience in maintaining such platform, as well as acknowledging my so-so fluency in writing content in English.

I will use this platform to reflect my struggle in finding my forte. The biggest questions that became the reason of making this website is "What is my purpose here? Why do I have such purpose?"

Therefore, I am quite sure that my post would be varied, I mean, abstractly varied. The content in this website might be not like an accomplished goal, but rather unfinished project and continued brainstorming.

Maybe by making this website, I would be able more fluent in describing a problem or giving appropriate action on other's problem. And finally.. I hope I can discover what makes me want to keep thriving the most.
